Work History

2011 - 2020

Packaging Corp of America

Machine Operator, Clamp/Fork Truck Driver

At PCA, I've played many roles. I've operated machines, loaded and unloaded tractor trailers and rail cars. I have kept track of inventory, stocked the presses and corrugator. I was responsible for the quality of every machine I worked on. I was on the PEO (Plant Emergency Organization) team as well as a union steward/rep. I was also part of multiple committees such as the waste committee, the survey response team, and more.

2009 - 2011

Pizza Hut

Server

At Pizza Hut, my title was server but that never stopped me from learning more. I was always helping with clean up, food prep, making pizzas and the occasional customer complaint. I was opening the resteraunt some days, and closing it others. I treated EVERY customer as if they were my own family and always made sure their food was spot on, if not better, than what they were expecting. Working with customers has taught me patience and the importance of a quality product in a timely manner. It was one of my regular customers that saw my potential and worth who got me a job at PCA.

2008 - 2009

Variety of Temp Jobs

Farms, Small Factories, Construction, Roofing, Plumbing

After being discharged from the Navy, I immediately found work doing odd jobs. I've split wood, worked at multiple factories through temp agencies, did a little roofing and plumbing, etc... I was learning more skills and realizing the drive it requires to be successful in the real world. I learned to never give and never be too humble to work a job.
